INSIDE OUT 2

Jan 4th 2024 8 Min read

Inside Out 2: Exploring New Emotions

Inside Out returns in the highly anticipated follow up, Inside Out 2, which takes place several years later inside Riley’s mind as a teenager where her emotions are at an all time high. With new emotions being introduced such as anxiety, nostalgia and self-doubt, Riley now has to learn how to control and deal with these newfound feelings. These emotions are now expected to help Riley get through the challenging teenage phase where every day is a struggle.

In my view, Inside Out 2 is much better than the first one because it has so much emotional depth. The nostalgia surrounding the film does not overshadow the mental health aspect during its development. The documentary depicts the deeply rooted changes that take place in one’s emotion with age. Riley struggles with having more emotions than just happy, sad and angered which makes her easier to empathize with. The detached creativity is blended perfectly with the rest of the film, creating a humorous yet contemplative narrative suitable for children and adults alike.

Like with the first movie, the animation is breathtaking and the new character additions only bolster the original idea. This sequel serves as a reminder of how most people ignore and suppress their emotions, and instead encourages the audience with how kinder they can be to themselves while going through life.

Inside Out 2 also highlights the importance of self-awareness and emotional growth, encouraging viewers to embrace their complexity rather than fear it. The film’s approach to mental health, while still lighthearted, provides an accessible way for both young and adult audiences to better understand their feelings. It's a truly thoughtful sequel.
